How it works

From source selection to ready-to-boot USB in three clear stages

1) Analyze source and prepare target

Select your installer file and destination drive. macUSB validates requirements and gives you an immediate, readable summary.

2) Confirm the workflow

Review what will happen before creation begins: file preparation, USB formatting, copy sequence, and cleanup.

3) Monitor and finish

Follow stage-by-stage progress, then complete with guided post-install notes for your target machine generation.
